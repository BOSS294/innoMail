InnoMail V1.0

InnoMail is your one-stop solution for effortless email integration. Experience fast, secure, and easy-to-use features for sending professional emails.

Benefits

Quick integration, easy setup, and reliable email delivery with enhanced security.

Features

Supports Mailjet API, handles templates, and ensures smooth communication with your users.

How to Use?

Simply import InnoMail, call the `sendEmail()` function, and you're ready to go!
